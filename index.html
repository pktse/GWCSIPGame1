Very often both men and women are judged based on social norms,
Yet most people don't fall into these unrealistic traits.
These sterotypes prevent us from discovering
our true identities

LIvES is a way of discussing social norms in a simple way






We decided to make this game exposing expectations placed on people starting from
a young age to adulthood.

We hope this game raises awareness about how restrictive
societal norms and expectations are towards everyone.

Instructions

You will randomly be given either a boy or girl character. During the game, you will go through 3 stages; baby, teen, and adult. In each stage you will get points for collecting sterotypicial items your gender would like to expose the absurdity of societal norms. To move around, use the arrow keys.
